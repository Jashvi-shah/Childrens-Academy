Children's Academy

At Children’s Academy, we believe in providing a progressive learning environment that fosters joyful learning and holistic development. With state-of-the-art infrastructure, we serve over 8000 students across four campuses in Mumbai and Thane. Our mission is to empower students to become lifelong learners, ready to face the challenges of a dynamic world.

Mission

Children’s Academy Group of Schools aims at providing a progressive learning environment that leads to joyful learning along with holistic development. Our primary endeavour is to create passionate students who are lifelong learners, capable of accepting the dynamic challenges of the new world order. We have always been ahead of the curve in aligning with the National Education Policy (NEP) 2020, even before its official implementation. As a K12 school offering the ICSE curriculum from Nursery to Grade 10, we are committed to holistic development that nurtures curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity at every stage of a child’s educational journey. We take pride in our robust lesson planning, backed by a dedicated team of in-house academic researchers who continuously refine and enhance our curriculum. Our new-age teaching methods focus on experiential learning, critical thinking, and real-world applications, ensuring that students gain both knowledge and practical skills. With a strong emphasis on industry readiness, we equip students with the adaptability and confidence needed to excel in their chosen paths. In addition to our ICSE curriculum, we offer pathways such as the International Baccalaureate Career Programme (IBCP) to ensure that students are equipped for future success, whether they continue their journey in higher education or the professional world. Rooted in inquiry-based learning and interdisciplinary approaches, the IBCP fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and real-world application of knowledge. With a strong global perspective, our students gain exposure to international best practices, diverse cultures, and industry collaborations, preparing them to navigate an increasingly interconnected world with confidence and adaptability.
